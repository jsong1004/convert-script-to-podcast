Convert Audio Script to a Podcast

The "Convert Audio Script to a Podcast" application transforms written dialogue scripts into professional-sounding podcasts. Users can input scripts with multiple characters and a host, each with distinct voices. The app leverages Murf AI's text-to-speech technology to generate natural-sounding conversation, making it easy for content creators, educators, and storytellers to produce engaging audio content without the need for recording equipment or voice actors.
